The Best Veggie Quesadillas!

What is Quesadillas?

A quesadilla is a popular Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with cheese and other ingredients, folded, and cooked until the cheese melts. The name "Quesadilla" comes from the Spanish word "Queso", meaning cheese, reflecting the dish's core ingredient.

Let's Make Scrumptious Quesadillas!

Ingredients

  • (400g) Black beans
  • (150g) Cheddar cheese
  • 6 Tortillas
  • 1 large diced into cubes Red onion
  • 1 large diced into cubes Tomato
  • 1 cup diced into cubes Multi peppers
  • 1cup fresh or frozen and thawed Corn kernels
  • Half Bunch of Cilantro
  • Spice blend
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Chipotle Sauce

  • 3 tablespoon Greek yoghurt or mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Teaspoon chipotle in adobo sauce

Garnish

  • Lime or lemon(optional)

Instructions

  1. Dry fry corn on a pan on a medium heat without any oil to get them nice and charred. Once charred,remove corn to a bowl.

  2. Saut√© onion in olive oil on a medium heat, once cooked, add to the same bowl.

  3. Add beans, tomato, and coriander to the bowl, season with salt and pepper, mix to combine all ingredients.

  4. Mix chipotle sauce ingredients in a small bowl, you can adjust either adding more or less chipotle depending on how spicy you like it.

  5. In the same pan, add a tortilla (there is no need to add any oil).

  6. Add some sauce to half of the tortilla Spoon some of the mixture evenly on top of the sauce, still keeping on half of the tortilla. Then top with cheese.

  7. Using a spatula, fold the empty side of the quesadilla over and press down so that the cheese becomes like a glue to stick the tortilla together.

  8. After a minute, flip the tortilla over so the second side gets a nice golden color. Once golden remove off the pan and continue with the remaining tortillas.

  9. Slice quesadillas in half, squeeze some lemon or lime (optional) and dig in!!
